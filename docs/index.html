What we deliver

  • Creata Creator Academy (EN): wallet hygiene, metadata/royalties, collection design, pricing/allowlists, compliance basics, GTM, and post-drop ops.
  • Open starter kit (Catena/EVM): NFT preset, deploy scripts, example dApp (Connect → Mint), and Extension SDK demos (connect, accounts, tx).
  • Mini indexer + KPI dashboard: mints, active addresses, basic on-chain metrics with CSV/JSON export.
  • Public tutorials: “Mint on Catena (CIP-721)”, “Integrate Extension SDK”, and a Lunar Link showcase.

Education first

Three cohorts × 12 creators (year-long). Curriculum forged from real, hands-on launches. The goal is simple: onboard creators safely and turn learning into measurable on-chain outcomes.
